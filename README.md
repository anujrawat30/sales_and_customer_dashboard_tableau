# 📊 Sales & Customer Performance Analytics
The Sales & Customer Performance Analytics dashboards are visually engaging, dynamic and interactive dashboards designed to help sales managers, executives, and marketing teams analyze year-over-year sales performance, customer behavior, and business trends. The dashboards provide actionable insights into revenue growth, sales patterns, customer segments, and engagement metrics to support data-driven decision-making. 

## Porpose:
### 📈 Sales Dashboard – Purpose Summary

An interactive Tableau dashboard designed to monitor and analyze sales performance through KPI tracking, year-over-year comparisons, sales trends, product subcategory insights, and weekly sales & profit analysis. It helps stakeholders identify growth patterns, high-performing categories, and areas requiring business attention.

### 👥 Customer Dashboard – Purpose Summary

A customer analytics dashboard built to analyze customer behavior, engagement, and profitability through KPI monitoring, customer trend analysis, order distribution insights, and top customers performance tracking. It enables marketing teams and management to better understand customer segments and improve business strategies.

### 🛠️ Tech Stack
The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

* 🧠 **Calculated Fields:** Used to create custom fields and business metrics using existing dimensions and measures from the database.

* 🎛️ **Parameters:** Used alongside calculated fields to make visualizations dynamic and interactive for users.

* 📝 **Data Modeling:** Established relationships among tables such as Orders, Customers, and Products to enable accurate cross-filtering, aggregation, and analysis.

* 🔍 **Filters:** Used to sort and refine the data dynamically based on user selections and analysis requirements.

* 🔘 **Buttons:** Used for seamless navigation and switching between the Sales Dashboard and Customer Dashboard.

* 📊 **Dashboards:** Used to bring together KPIs, charts, filters, and insights into interactive and centralized views for sales and customer analysis.

### Data Source
📂 Enterprise Retail Sales Dataset- data academy
Comprehensive retail transaction dataset containing customer, order, and product-level data across five years (2018–2022). Includes key business metrics such as sales, profit, quantity, customer segments, geographic performance, product categories, and subcategories to support advanced sales and customer analytics.

### Key Features & Highlights
🧩 Business Problem Statement
Retail businesses often struggle to effectively monitor sales performance and customer behavior due to fragmented reporting and limited analytical visibility. Sales managers and executives face challenges in identifying revenue trends, tracking profitability, understanding customer purchasing patterns, and recognizing high-value customers for strategic decision-making.

The lack of interactive analytics makes it difficult to:
- Track year-over-year sales and profit performance
- Identify top and low-performing products and subcategories
- Monitor weekly sales and profit fluctuations
- Analyze customer engagement and purchasing behavior
- Identify loyal and high-profit customers
- Make faster, data-driven business decisions

To solve this problem, an interactive Tableau-based Sales & Customer Analytics Dashboard was developed to centralize KPIs, sales trends, profitability analysis, and customer insights into a single business intelligence solution.


❓ Key Questions Answered by the Dashboard

- How are sales, profit, and order quantities performing compared to the previous year?
- Which months recorded the highest and lowest sales performance?
- Which product subcategories generate the highest sales and profits?
- Which products or subcategories are underperforming?
- How do weekly sales and profit trends compare against average performance?
- How many customers and orders were generated year-over-year?
- What is the average sales value per customer?
- Which customers contribute the highest profit to the business?
- How are customers distributed based on purchase frequency?
- How does performance vary across categories, regions, states, and cities?


🎯 Goal of the Dashboard

The goal of this Tableau-based Sales & Customer Analytics Dashboard is to provide an interactive and centralized platform for analyzing sales performance, customer behavior, and business trends. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor KPIs, track year-over-year growth, identify profitable products and customers, and make data-driven decisions through dynamic visualizations and interactive analytics.

📊 Dashboard Walkthrough

📈 Sales Dashboard
- **KPI Cards:** Displays Total Sales, Profit, and Quantity with YoY comparison and monthly trend indicators.
- **Monthly Trend Analysis:** Line charts comparing current vs previous year sales and profit trends.
- **Product Subcategory Analysis:** Bar charts comparing sales and profit performance across product subcategories.
- **Weekly Performance Trends:** Weekly sales and profit analysis with average benchmark reference lines.

👥 Customer Dashboard
- **Customer KPI Cards:** Displays Total Customers, Orders, and Sales per Customer with YoY insights.
- **Customer Trend Analysis:** Monthly customer performance comparison across years.
- **Customer Distribution Analysis:** Distribution chart showing customer order frequency and engagement patterns.
- **Top 10 Customers:** Ranked table highlighting the most profitable customers with sales, profit, and order details.

🎛️ Interactive Features
- Dynamic filters for category, subcategory, region, state, and city.
- Navigation buttons for switching between dashboards.
- Historical year selection for trend and comparative analysis.


📊 Charts & Visualizations Used

- **KPI Cards with Sparklines:** Used for displaying key metrics with compact trend visualization and YoY comparison.
- **Line Charts:** Used to analyze monthly sales, profit, and customer trends over time.
- **Horizontal Bar Charts:** Used for comparing sales and profit performance across product subcategories.
- **Step Line Charts:** Used to visualize weekly sales and profit fluctuations against average benchmarks.
- **Reference Lines:** Used to highlight average sales and profit performance levels.
- **Distribution Chart / Histogram:** Used to analyze customer order frequency and purchasing behavior.
- **Ranked Table:** Used to display Top 10 Customers by Profit with detailed customer metrics.
- **Interactive Filters:** Used for dynamic drill-down analysis across categories and locations.
- **Navigation Buttons:** Used for seamless switching between Sales and Customer dashboards.
